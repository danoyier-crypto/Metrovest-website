About This Cover

What Is Marine Cover?

Metrovest sources marine cargo insurance from licensed underwriters experienced in East African trade routes, and manages open cover arrangements for regular importers and exporters. We advise on the correct Institute Cargo Clause, review shipping documents, issue certificates of insurance and handle claim notifications with the insurer — so your supply chain never stops.
