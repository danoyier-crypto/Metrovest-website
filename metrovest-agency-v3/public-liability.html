About This Cover

What Is Public Liability Insurance?

Metrovest sources public liability cover from general insurers with responsive legal defence teams. As an independent agency, we compare limit adequacy, policy extensions and premium rates across the market — and ensure your policy wording is appropriate for your specific business type, whether retail, hospitality, events or construction.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Bodily injury or death to third parties on your premises
  • ✓Property damage to third-party property
  • ✓Legal defence costs
  • ✓Medical expenses for injured third parties
  • ✓Products liability extension for manufacturers and retailers
  • ✓Pollution liability extension (limited)
  • ✓Tenant's liability to landlord
  • ✓Contractual liability where legally required
