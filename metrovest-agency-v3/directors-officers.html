What Is Directors & Officers (D&O) Insurance?

Metrovest sources Directors & Officers liability cover from specialist liability underwriters and Lloyd's-backed facilities available in the Kenyan market. As an independent advisor, we compare Side A, B and C structures, retroactive dates and limit adequacy — and explain the policy in plain language to your board before you commit.
