About This Cover

What Is WIBA?

Metrovest sources WIBA cover from IRA-licensed general insurance underwriters and manages the placement, endorsement and claims process on your behalf. As your intermediary, we ensure your WIBA policy is legally compliant with the Work Injury Benefits Act 2007, correctly rated against your payroll, and placed with an underwriter with a strong claims settlement record.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Medical expenses for work-related injuries
  • ✓Permanent total disability — up to 96 months' salary
  • ✓Permanent partial disability — proportionate benefit
  • ✓Temporary total disability — weekly wage replacement
  • ✓Death benefit — lump sum to dependants
  • ✓Occupational disease cover
  • ✓Transportation of injured employee
  • ✓Legal defence costs (employer liability extension)
