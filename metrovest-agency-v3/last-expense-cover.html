About This Cover

What Is Last Expense Cover?

Metrovest sources last expense cover from established Kenyan underwriters that have a proven record of fast, dignified payouts. We compare payout speeds, premium rates and eligible dependants across the market so your family is never left negotiating with an insurer in their most vulnerable moment.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Lump sum payout on death of insured or covered dependants
  • ✓Spouse cover included in family plans
  • ✓Children cover from birth (38 weeks gestation)
  • ✓Extended family cover — parents, in-laws, siblings (optional)
  • ✓No waiting period for accidental death
  • ✓30-day general waiting period for natural death
  • ✓Covers transport of remains upcountry or internationally
  • ✓Hospital mortuary fees sub-limit
