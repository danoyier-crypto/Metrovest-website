What Is Professional Indemnity Insurance?

Metrovest sources professional indemnity insurance from underwriters experienced in covering Kenyan professionals across law, medicine, engineering, accountancy, IT and financial services. We compare policy wordings, retroactive dates and limit adequacy across the market, then recommend the most appropriate cover — and manage all claim notifications on your behalf.
