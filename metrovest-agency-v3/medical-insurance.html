What Is Medical Insurance?

Medical emergencies can strike without warning, and the cost of quality healthcare in Kenya continues to rise. As an insurance agency, Metrovest searches the market and handpicks medical cover options from Kenya's leading underwriters — including Jubilee Health, Britam, CIC, and AAR — to bring you the most comprehensive inpatient, outpatient and specialist plans at the most competitive premiums.
