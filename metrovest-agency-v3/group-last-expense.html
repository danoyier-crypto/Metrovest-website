About This Cover

What Is Group Last Expense Cover?

Metrovest sources group last expense schemes from established underwriters and manages the policy on behalf of employers, SACCOs and chamas. We negotiate per-member premiums, manage additions and deletions, and personally ensure that bereaved families receive their payout quickly — acting as the liaison between your organisation and the underwriter.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Death of principal employee
  • ✓Spouse cover
  • ✓Dependent children cover
  • ✓Optional extended family cover — parents, in-laws
  • ✓No waiting period for accidental death
  • ✓30-day general waiting period for natural death (new entrants)
