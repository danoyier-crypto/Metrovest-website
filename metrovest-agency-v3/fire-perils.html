About This Cover

What Is Fire & Perils Insurance?

Metrovest sources fire and allied perils cover from general insurance underwriters with strong property claims track records. We conduct a sum insured review, advise on reinstatement versus market value basis, and include business interruption assessment — ensuring that in the event of a fire, your claim settlement reflects the true cost of rebuilding and restoring your business.
