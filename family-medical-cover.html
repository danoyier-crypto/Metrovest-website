About This Cover

What Is Family Medical Cover?

Metrovest acts as your independent insurance advisor, sourcing family medical cover from top-rated Kenyan underwriters such as Jubilee Health, Britam and CIC. We compare plans across the market, negotiate on your behalf and recommend the most suitable and affordable cover for your household — from newborns to seniors — under a single annual policy.
