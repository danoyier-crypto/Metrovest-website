About This Cover

What Is a Group Life Policy?

Metrovest acts as intermediary to source group life assurance from Kenya's most financially sound life underwriters. We tender your scheme, negotiate benefit multiples and premiums, manage staff enrolment and handle all claim notifications — giving your HR team a single point of contact rather than dealing with an insurer directly.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Death benefit — typically 2–5× annual salary
  • ✓Permanent total disability (PTD) benefit
  • ✓Terminal illness benefit
  • ✓Funeral expense allowance
  • ✓Optional accidental death double benefit
  • ✓Optional education benefit for surviving children
  • ✓Optional critical illness rider
