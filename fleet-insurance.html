About This Cover

What Is Fleet Insurance?

Metrovest manages fleet insurance programmes for organisations across Kenya by sourcing cover from underwriters who specialise in fleet accounts. We consolidate all your vehicles onto a single policy, negotiate fleet discounts, track No Claims Discounts per vehicle and handle all claims — giving your finance and logistics teams a completely managed insurance programme.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Comprehensive cover for all fleet vehicles — sedans, SUVs, pickups, trucks, buses
  • ✓Third-party bodily injury and death (unlimited)
  • ✓Third-party property damage
  • ✓Accidental damage, fire and theft per vehicle
  • ✓Windscreen and glass cover
  • ✓24/7 roadside assistance and recovery
  • ✓Driver personal accident cover
  • ✓Medical expenses for driver and passengers
