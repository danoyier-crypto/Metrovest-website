What Is Domestic Package Insurance?

As an independent agency, Metrovest sources domestic package insurance from underwriters who provide the broadest home and contents cover at the fairest premiums. We compare buildings cover, all-risks contents, burglary limits and liability across the market — whether you own or rent your home — and recommend only what you actually need.

  • ✓All-risks household contents
  • ✓Burglary and housebreaking — cash and valuables

  • ✓Personal accident for domestic staff (WIBA compliant)
