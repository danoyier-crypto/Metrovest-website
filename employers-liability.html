About This Cover

What Is Employers Liability Insurance?

Metrovest sources employers liability (common law) cover from underwriters with experienced legal defence capabilities. We place this alongside your WIBA cover and ensure the two policies work together seamlessly — so that whether a claim arises under statute or common law, you have the right cover in place and a dedicated intermediary managing the process.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Common law damages awarded to injured employees
  • ✓Legal defence and representation costs
  • ✓Appeals and settlement costs
  • ✓Diseases contracted due to employment conditions
  • ✓Claims by former employees relating to their period of service
  • ✓Punitive and exemplary damages where insurable at law
