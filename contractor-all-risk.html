About This Cover

What Is Contractor All Risk Insurance?

Metrovest sources Contractor All Risk insurance from specialist construction underwriters and manages the placement on behalf of contractors, developers and project owners. We review your contract documents, advise on the correct sum insured and structure the policy to ensure full coverage of the works, plant and third-party liability — then manage the insurer relationship throughout the project.

Coverage

What's Covered

  • ✓Physical loss or damage to the contract works
  • ✓Contractor's plant, equipment and machinery
  • ✓Third-party bodily injury and property damage liability
  • ✓Surrounding property damage
  • ✓Existing structures on site
  • ✓Removal of debris following an insured event
  • ✓Defects liability period extension (up to 12 months)
  • ✓Advance loss of profits (ALOP) for project owner (optional)
